How to use this site:

Select a time range on the frequency chart (bottom left) to select asteroid that passed near Earth at that time. The two bars next to that are used to filter by attributes. The two charts on the right show attributes, and highlighting a data node on any chart will show you the corresponding values for the same asteroids on other charts.
